History

In 2010, TechCon began as a grassroots event for computer lovers. It has developed over time into one of the leading international technology conferences, drawing thousands of people each year.

Mission

Our goal at TechCon is to encourage creativity, teamwork, and knowledge sharing among the international tech community. We are committed to creating a technologically enabled future that is advantageous to all.
